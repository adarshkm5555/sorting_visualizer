What is FitNetwork?

FitNetwork is a boutique fitness studio in Fredericksburg, Virginia that offers group fitness classes in "Sixty", our original concept HIIT class, Cycle, Barre, Row, and Yoga. In addition to group classes, we launched a "Sweat Lab" that allows you to conduct individual training, on your own, or with a personal trainer whenever Sweat is open.
