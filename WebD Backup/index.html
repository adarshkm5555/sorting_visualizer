Welcome to Sorting Visualizer

A sorting visualizer is a tool or program that allows users to visually observe and understand how different sorting algorithms work.

Sorting Visualizer

A sorting visualizer typically provides a graphical representation of the sorting process, allowing users to see how the elements are manipulated and rearranged step by step. It often includes animations or visual cues to demonstrate the comparisons and swaps made by the sorting algorithm.
